This applcation is all about creating an online platform that facilitates tracking
vaccination, health and growth metrics of an infant (0 to 60 months). The application
shall facilitate the below high level requiriements 
 
 1. Parents are enrolled when the mother gets admited in a maternity ward.
 2. Track parents' health checkup data and delivery complications and measures.
 3. Track baby birth time related medico parameters.
 4. Generate the baby vaccination schedule.
 5. A reminder shall be triggered on the event of a vaccine due approach(a week earlier).
 6. Vaccination given shall be recorded.
 7. Baby growth parameters shall be recorded. 
 8. Billing Services.
